Hotate and
Ika Nigiri

Time: 30 min

Serving Size: 6

Yield: 12 pieces

Ingredients
  • 200g fresh scallop (without shell)
  • 200g fresh squid
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi (optional)
Steps
  1. Prepare the rice
    Cook sushi rice as per package instructions and after cooled, stir in vinegar, salt and sugar, and set aside to cool for at least 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare scallop
    Rinse the scallops under cold water and pat dry. Remove and discard scallop side muscle and slice horizontally into thin slices, about 6mm thick
  3. Prepare squid
    Rinse squid under cold water and pat dry. If squid is whole, slice open,remove internal organs and peel of membrane from outside. Cut into thin slices about 6mm thick.
  4. Assemble
    Take a 2 tablespoons sushi rice and form it into an oval-shaped mound using wet hands. Place sliced scallop or squid, pressing it lightly to adhere.
  5. Serve
    Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi
