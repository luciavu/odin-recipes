Ikura
Ship

Time: 15 min

Serving Size: 6

Yield: 24 rolls

Ingredients
  • Ikura (salmon roe)
  • 1 cup cooked sushi rice
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi (optional)
Steps
  1. Prepare the rice
    Cook sushi rice as per package instructions and after cooled, stir in vinegar, salt and sugar, and set aside to cool for at least 5 minutes.
  2. Assemble
    Take a 2 tablespoons sushi rice and form it into an oval-shaped mound using wet hands. Place ikura on top of rice, pressing it lightly to adhere.
  3. Wrap nori
    Cut nori into small sheets and wrap around mound.
  4. Serve
    Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi
