Soy
Sauce

Time: 35 min

Serving Size: 1

Yield: 1 cup

Ingredients
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
Steps
  1. Mix the ingredients
    In small bowl, combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, mirin, sesame oil, ginger, garlic and brown sugar. Mix until sugar dissolves.
  2. Leave to sit
    Allow soy sauce to sit for at least 30 minutes for flavours to meld together
  3. Strain
    If you prefer smooth soy sauce, strain mixture to remove ginger and garlic.
