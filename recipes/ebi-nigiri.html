Ebi
Nigiri

Time: 30 min

Serving Size: 6

Yield: 12 pieces

Ingredients
  • 12 shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi (optional)
Steps
  1. Prepare the rice
    Cook sushi rice as per package instructions and after cooled, stir in vinegar, salt and sugar, and set aside to cool for at least 5 minutes.
  2. Boil the shrimp
    Boil a pot of water cook the peeled and deveined shrimp for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Transfer the shrimp into a bowl of ice water until cooled, then pat shrimp dry.
  3. Assemble
    Take a 2 tablespoons sushi rice and form it into an oval-shaped mound using wet hands. Place a cooked shrimp on top of the rice mound, pressing it lightly to adhere.
  4. Serve
    Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi
