Cucumber
Roll

Time: 30 min

Serving Size: 6

Yield: 24 rolls

Ingredients
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 cup cooked sushi rice
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 nori sheets
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi (optional)
Steps
  1. Prepare the rice
    Cook sushi rice as per package instructions and after cooled, stir in vinegar, salt and sugar, and set aside to cool for at least 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the cucmber
    Cut the cucumber to the exact size of a sheet of nori and remove seeds from the center with a spoon. Slice the cucumber lengthwise into strips 1cm thick.
  3. Assemble the roll
    Cover a bamboo sushi mat with plastic wrap to keep rice from sticking. Place one nori sheet and spread rice evenly on it, leaving ~1cm uncovered. Arrange 3-4 cucumber in middle of sheet.
  4. Roll
    Lift the mat, roll over cucumber once, and press down. Unroll, then roll again towards the uncovered end of the nori to make a long roll. Seal roll with a little water if necessary. Repeat to make remaining rolls.
  5. Slice and serve
    Slice each roll into 6 pieces and serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger or wasabi.
